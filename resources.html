Essential Internet Resources

  • Have I Been Pwned Check if your email or passwords have been exposed in any major known data breaches. Enter your email to get instant results. Credit: Troy Hunt
  • Google Password Manager Securely generate and store strong, unique passwords for your accounts. Built into Google Chrome and Google accounts. Credit: Google LLC
  • Google Safe Browsing Safe Browsing is a Google service that lets client applications check URLs against Google's constantly updated lists of unsafe web resources.

    With Safe Browsing you can:

    • Check pages against our Safe Browsing lists based on platform and threat types.
    • Warn users before they click links in your site that may lead to infected pages.
    • Prevent users from posting links to known infected pages from your site.
    Credit: Google
  • VirusTotal Scan files or URLs for malware using dozens of antivirus engines. A quick way to check suspicious files and links. Credit: Google / Chronicle Security
  • SSL Labs SSL Test Analyze the SSL security of any website. Understand if a site uses up-to-date encryption. Credit: Qualys SSL Labs
  • Privacy Badger A browser extension that blocks trackers and helps keep your internet activity more private. Credit: Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF)
  • Stay Safe Online Practical tips, guides, and resources for all ages on privacy, passwords, scams, and online safety. Credit: National Cybersecurity Alliance
  • Cyber Aware UK UK government portal for everyday cybersecurity advice and learning for individuals and small businesses. Credit: UK National Cyber Security Centre
  • SANS Cybersecurity Awareness Training Free and paid cybersecurity awareness modules for employees, families, and lifelong learners. Credit: SANS Institute
  • CISA: Be Cyber Smart U.S. government resource center with guides, infographics, and toolkits for staying safe online. Credit: CISA
  • Cybersecurity for Beginners: The Basics Animated, easy-to-follow introduction to cybersecurity for total beginners. Credit: Simplilearn (YouTube)
  • Help Students Stay Safe Online Educate students on internet safety, privacy, and digital citizenship. Credit: Google for Education (YouTube)
  • ISEA - Stay Safe Online Information Security Education and Awareness (ISEA) is an initiative of Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India for generating human resources in the area of Information Security and creating general awareness on Cyber Hygiene/Cyber Security among the masses. Credit: ISEA
  • Krebs on Security Independent news, analysis, and investigations of security threats, breaches, and cybercrime. Credit: Brian Krebs
  • Dark Reading Latest IT security news, threat intelligence, and expert perspectives. Credit: Informa PLC
  • CyberScoop Regularly updated cybersecurity news including data breaches and security company news. Credit: Scoop News Group
  • Threatpost Up-to-date articles and news on malware, breaches, vulnerabilities, and more. Credit: Threatpost
